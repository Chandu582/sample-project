Our Commitment

At St. Teresa English School, we understand that entrusting us with your child's education also means entrusting us with sensitive personal information. We take this responsibility seriously. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect data in compliance with Indian data protection regulations.

1. Information We Collect

We collect necessary information to provide educational services and ensure campus safety:

  • Student Data: Name, Date of Birth, Blood Group, Medical history (if critical).
  • Parent/Guardian Data: Names, Contact Numbers, Email, and Residential Address for communication.
  • Academic Records: Previous school reports, Transfer Certificates, and ongoing performance data.

2. Use of Student Photos (Media Policy)

St. Teresa English School celebrates the achievements and vibrant life of our students. By enrolling your ward, you acknowledge that the school may use photographs or short video clips of your child for:

  • School Website: Displaying 'Toppers lists', 'Star Performers', 'Birthday corner', and event galleries.
  • Official Publications: School prospectus, newsletters, or educational boards within the campus.

Opt-Out Provision: If a parent/guardian has a strong objection to a specific image being displayed, please submit a written request to the Principal's office, and we will take necessary action.

3. Data Security & Storage

We utilize secure digital infrastructure (including trusted cloud services like Google Firebase) to store electronic records. Access to digital and physical student records is strictly restricted to authorized administrative and academic staff only.

4. Contact for Privacy Concerns

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or wish to update your data, please contact the school administration office during working hours.
