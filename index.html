About Us

Welcome to ST. TERESA ENGLISH SCHOOL, a dedicated educational institution nestled in the heart of Lalganj, Chandi, Nalanda (Bihar). Founded on the belief that every child is a precious gift with limitless potential, we are committed to providing an education that extends far beyond traditional classroom learning.

Our school is a vibrant, inclusive learning community where students are encouraged to discover their unique talents in a safe and stimulating environment. By blending modern teaching methodologies with deeply rooted moral values, we focus on the holistic development of every child—academically, socially, emotionally, and physically.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of educational excellence that empowers every student to become a compassionate, innovative, and responsible lifelong learner, deeply rooted in strong moral values and equipped to lead with confidence.

We aspire to create a dynamic and inclusive learning ecosystem where curiosity is celebrated, individual potential is nurtured, and children are prepared to positively shape a rapidly changing world.

Our Mission

  • Foster Holistic Growth: Safe, nurturing, and dynamic learning environment.
  • Empower Students: Modern academic practices with strong ethical roots.
  • Build Partnerships: Hand-in-hand with parents and the community.
  • Cultivate Leadership: Instilling essential life skills and social responsibility.

Our Core Values

The guiding principles that shape our students' character

Integrity

We believe in honesty, transparency, and doing the right thing, even when no one is watching.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in academics, extracurriculars, and personal conduct.

Compassion

We foster a culture of kindness, empathy, and respect for all individuals.

Resilience

We teach our students to embrace challenges, learn from failures, and never give up.
